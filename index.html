Privacy Policy - Dipisha's Art Corner Analytics

Last updated: 14 December 2025

Creator Analytics (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is a personal analytics dashboard operated by DIPISHA SHIVANGI / Dipisha's Art Corner. This page explains how we handle information when you connect social media accounts such as YouTube, Pinterest, and Instagram to the dashboard.

Information We Access

When you connect a platform, we access only the data provided through that platform’s official APIs, such as:

  • Channel, account, or profile identifiers
  • Analytics data like views, impressions, reach, clicks, and subscriber counts

We do not collect your passwords, and we do not post content on your behalf.

How We Use Data

The data we access is used solely to display analytics inside the Creator Analytics dashboard for your own use. We do not sell your data or share it with unrelated third parties.

Data Storage and Retention

Some analytics data may be stored in our database to show trends over time. You can disconnect a platform at any time, which stops new data from being fetched. You may also request deletion of stored data by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Our app relies on third-party services such as Google (YouTube), Pinterest, and Meta (Instagram). Your use of those services is also governed by their respective terms and privacy policies.
